## KEY MESSAGING PRINCIPLES

1. **Focus on outcomes, not technology:** Advance warning, margin protection, P&L impact. Not satellite imagery or technical methodology.

2. **Business language:** Talk about disruptions, margins, supplier adjustments, P&L accountability. No jargon.

3. **Proprietary but vague:** "Science-based approach," "proprietary methodology." Don't explain the how.

4. **Status transparency:** "Prototype phase," "backtesting validated the methodology." Shows credibility and honesty.

5. **Visitor inquiry:** Save the technical details for demos and pilots. Site shows business value only.

6. **Specific outcomes:** "6-12 months advance warning," "30-40% premiums on emergency sourcing," "millions at stake."

7. **Competitive advantage:** Not a tool—a strategic foresight advantage. Turns disruption into opportunity.
