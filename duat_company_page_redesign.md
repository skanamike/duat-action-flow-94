## ğŸ“Š CRITICAL ISSUES IDENTIFIED

### 1. **Length & Cognitive Load**
- **Problem:** 6 pages worth of content on a single page creates scroll fatigue
- **Impact:** Visitors bounce before understanding your value proposition
- **Solution:** Split into 4 distinct pages with clear navigation

### 2. **Buried Differentiators**
- **Problem:** Your most impressive tech ("patented subsurface SAR intelligence," "months ahead detection") is hidden in bullet points
- **Impact:** Investors/clients miss your competitive moats
- **Solution:** Hero stats and visual callouts for game-changing claims

### 3. **Dense Team Section**
- **Problem:** 8 detailed bios create a wall of text
- **Impact:** Decision-makers skim past valuable credibility signals
- **Solution:** Separate team page with visual grid + expandable details

### 4. **No Visual Anchors**
- **Problem:** All text, no infographics, charts, or imagery
- **Impact:** Fails to engage visual learners; looks generic
- **Solution:** Strategic infographics for technical concepts + team photos

## ğŸ”¤ TYPOGRAPHY & READABILITY IMPROVEMENTS

### **Current Problems:**
- Long paragraphs (especially in About section)
- No visual hierarchy in Market Differentiation bullets
- Important terms (CASCADE, SAR) don't stand out

### **Recommendations:**

#### **Headlines:**
- **Font:** Bold, modern sans-serif (Inter, Poppins, or Montserrat)
- **Size:** 48-64px for H1, 36-48px for H2
- **Color:** Dark navy or black

#### **Body Copy:**
- **Font:** Readable sans-serif (Inter or System UI)
- **Size:** 18-20px (current web standard)
- **Line height:** 1.6-1.8 (improves readability)
- **Max width:** 65-75 characters per line

#### **Emphasis:**
- **Bold key terms:** CASCADE, patented, months ahead, subsurface SAR
- **Color accents:** Teal/blue for clickable elements and highlights
- **Whitespace:** 50-100px between major sections

#### **Paragraph Length:**
- **Max:** 3-4 sentences per paragraph
- **Current "About" paragraph:** 4 sentences â†’ split into 2 paragraphs
